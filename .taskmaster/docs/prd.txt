# Maidly.ai Strategic Development Plan - Product Requirements Document

## Executive Summary

This PRD outlines the strategic development roadmap for Maidly.ai to transform from a solid MVP into a market-leading, AI-powered cleaning service platform. The plan focuses on four phases over 16 weeks, prioritizing revenue acceleration, operational excellence, market expansion, and platform maturity.

## Business Objectives

### Primary Goals
- Increase revenue by 40% in Phase 1 (3 weeks)
- Achieve 60% operational efficiency improvement in Phase 2 (3 weeks)  
- Enable 25% revenue optimization through AI in Phase 3 (4 weeks)
- Establish platform maturity for enterprise market in Phase 4 (6 weeks)

### Success Metrics
- Customer satisfaction: 4.5 → 4.8+ stars
- Booking conversion rate: 15% → 25%
- Repeat customer rate: 45% → 70%
- Average order value: $200 → $275
- Crew efficiency: 85% → 95% on-time completion

## Current State Analysis

### Strengths (Industry-Leading)
- Core operations: 90% complete and functional
- AI integration: Unique competitive advantage with preference learning
- Payroll system: Industry-leading Stripe Connect integration
- Hiring pipeline: 11 real applications, professional tokenized system
- Database architecture: Production-ready, comprehensive schema

### Critical Gaps (Revenue Impact)
- Photo documentation: Missing industry-standard feature
- Real-time notifications: No SMS/email automation
- Advanced scheduling: No drag-and-drop interface
- Analytics visualization: Placeholder charts need real data
- Customer communication: Missing in-app messaging

## Phase 1: Revenue Acceleration (Weeks 1-3)

### Objective
Convert leads to customers and improve operational efficiency through customer-facing enhancements.

### Priority 1.1: Photo Documentation System
**Business Impact:** CRITICAL - Industry standard requirement
**Timeline:** Week 1 (5 days)

**Requirements:**
- Crew mobile interface for before/after photo capture
- GPS metadata embedding in photos for location verification
- Photo gallery integration in admin dashboard
- Automated photo delivery to customers via email
- Photo storage optimization and CDN integration
- Quality validation and compression algorithms

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Crew can capture photos with one-tap interface
- Photos automatically tagged with job ID, timestamp, GPS coordinates
- Admin can view all photos organized by job and date
- Customers receive photo gallery within 1 hour of service completion
- Photo loading time under 2 seconds on mobile devices

### Priority 1.2: Real-Time Notification System
**Business Impact:** CRITICAL - Customer communication and retention
**Timeline:** Week 1-2 (7 days)

**Requirements:**
- SMS notifications for booking confirmations and crew arrival alerts
- Email automation for service completion and follow-up sequences
- Push notifications for admin dashboard real-time updates
- Customer communication preference management
- Notification delivery tracking and retry mechanisms
- Multi-language notification support

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Customers receive SMS within 30 seconds of booking confirmation
- Crew arrival notifications sent 15 minutes before arrival
- Email sequences trigger automatically based on service status
- Admin receives real-time alerts for urgent issues
- 99% notification delivery success rate

### Priority 1.3: Enhanced Booking Management Interface
**Business Impact:** HIGH - Admin efficiency and scheduling optimization
**Timeline:** Week 2-3 (8 days)

**Requirements:**
- Drag-and-drop calendar interface for job scheduling
- Visual crew assignment with availability indicators
- Booking conflict detection and resolution suggestions
- Bulk operations for multiple booking management
- Real-time schedule synchronization across devices
- Integration with crew mobile app for schedule updates

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Admin can reschedule jobs with drag-and-drop in under 10 seconds
- Booking conflicts highlighted with resolution suggestions
- Crew schedules update in real-time on mobile devices
- Bulk operations handle 50+ bookings simultaneously
- Schedule changes sync across all devices within 5 seconds

### Priority 1.4: Analytics Visualization Dashboard
**Business Impact:** HIGH - Data-driven decision making
**Timeline:** Week 3 (5 days)

**Requirements:**
- Revenue trend charts with monthly/weekly/daily views
- Customer satisfaction metrics with trend analysis
- Crew performance analytics and benchmarking
- Booking pattern analysis with predictive insights
- Interactive filtering and drill-down capabilities
- Automated report generation and scheduling

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Charts load with real data in under 3 seconds
- Interactive filters update visualizations instantly
- Reports can be exported in PDF/Excel formats
- Automated weekly reports sent to stakeholders
- Mobile-responsive dashboard for on-the-go access

## Phase 2: Operational Excellence (Weeks 4-6)

### Objective
Scale operations and improve crew efficiency through advanced management tools.

### Priority 2.1: Route Optimization System
**Business Impact:** HIGH - Cost reduction and crew efficiency
**Timeline:** Week 4-5 (8 days)

**Requirements:**
- Google Maps API integration for intelligent routing
- Automatic crew assignment based on location and skills
- Real-time traffic consideration and route adjustments
- Travel time calculation with buffer management
- Multi-stop optimization for crew with multiple jobs
- Integration with crew mobile app for turn-by-turn navigation

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Routes optimized to reduce travel time by 20%
- Crew assignments consider location within 5-mile radius
- Real-time traffic updates adjust routes automatically
- Navigation integration launches directly from crew app
- Route efficiency reports available for performance analysis

### Priority 2.2: In-App Communication System
**Business Impact:** HIGH - Field operations and emergency response
**Timeline:** Week 5-6 (8 days)

**Requirements:**
- Real-time chat between admin, crew, and customers
- Emergency contact protocols with escalation procedures
- Job-specific communication threads with history
- File sharing capabilities for photos and documents
- Message read receipts and delivery confirmations
- Integration with existing notification system

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Messages delivered in real-time with under 2-second latency
- Emergency alerts reach all relevant parties within 30 seconds
- Chat history preserved and searchable by job/date
- File uploads support images, PDFs, and documents up to 10MB
- Offline message queuing with automatic sync when online

### Priority 2.3: Customer Self-Service Portal
**Business Impact:** HIGH - Customer retention and admin workload reduction
**Timeline:** Week 6 (5 days)

**Requirements:**
- Customer login and profile management interface
- Service history with photo galleries and notes
- One-click rebooking with preference memory
- Schedule modification and cancellation capabilities
- Preference settings and special request management
- Integration with AI system for personalized recommendations

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- Customers can log in and access full service history
- Rebooking process completed in under 60 seconds
- Schedule changes reflected in admin system immediately
- AI recommendations improve booking conversion by 15%
- Customer portal reduces admin support requests by 40%
